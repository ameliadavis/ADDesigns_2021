Client Need

A friend reached out about building a website to showcase her paintings. She had worked with a local print shop for some framing and wanted to keep continuity with the same font.

My Solution

I developed branding including patterns, colors and logo. To help potential customers better visualize I created mock ups for each of her framed paintings in rooms where they were best displayed.

Logo Design / Typography / Branding / Brand Guidelines / Photoshop / Illustrator/Indesign / AdobeXD

Additional Project Info

I expanded the overall design package with custom business cards and a brand design guide. I hope to grow the brand and her reach with future email campaigns.
