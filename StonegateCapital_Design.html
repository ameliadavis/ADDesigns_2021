Stonegate Capital Management

Client Need

As a new business Stonegate needed branding and a website to help them get started. They had identified their ideal client as an experienced investor so that was my focus in this process.

My Solution

I started by building a logo that would inspire confidence and be timeless. The client wanted to include a castle-like structure and wanted an icon that matched their name. I used a serif font that was very balanced and expanded the tracking to put the focus of the logo on the company name.

Website Design /Logo Design / Branding / Typography / Photoshop / Illustrator / AdobeXD / Wordpress

Additional Project Info
