Here in MS

April 2020 - present

What the client needed

A small bank in Mississippi was looking to grow their audience, our team at mnemonic agency proposed the #hereInMS hashtag for social media and television commercial. Since they already had established brand similar colors and styles were used for #HereInMS.

My part of the project

As part of the campaign for #HereInMS the client needed a landing page from the commercial and to help drive traffic. I developed this site using HTML, CSS and PHP. To help with social engagement users can submit media to be featured on their feed.

Vist Website

Additional Project Info

The landing page also features a feed of the clients current social media that they can curate with a third party software. I also contributed some social media posts to promote their new site.
